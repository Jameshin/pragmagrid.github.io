Members and Partners

PRAGMA is an institution-based organization. Institutions and members at those institutions provide resources to participate in working groups, and agree to host PRAGMA workshops. There are varied benefits accruing to participating institutions.

Active participation is the key to membership. We encourage interested individuals to attend a working meeting prior to discussion of membership at the institutional level. Please see a list of Additional Organizations active in PRAGMA.
