Members and Partners

PRAGMA is an institution-based organization. Institutions and members at those institutions provide resources to participate in working groups, and agree to host PRAGMA workshops. There are varied benefits accruing to participating institutions. Since membership is at institutional level; benefits include setting future directions of research and development, testing new software and technologies, accessing resources for development and application, providing students opportunities for research and leadership opportunities, hosting workshops, building longer-term collaborations, and much more. To learn more, see benefits.

Active participation is the key to membership. We encourage interested individuals to attend a working meeting prior to discussion of membership at the institutional level.
