Venue 

 PRAGMA 31 Workshop will be held in S31 Sukhumvit Hotel Bangkok on Sukhumvit Road. It takes approximately 5 minutes’ walk from Phrom Phong BTS Station, and 20.6 km from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Visa Information

PRAGMA attendees from many countries will be required to obtain a visa to enter Thailand.  Please consult the summary of countries entitled for Visa exemption and visa on arrival to Thailand at Department of Consular Affairs website


Transportation

 By Airport Limousine Service

 You may book a trip with AOT Limousine by sending an email to aotlimousine@yahoo.co.th with the following information:

  1.    Your name
  2.    Your flight number and arrival time
  3.    Vehicle type (Toyota Camry, ISUZU MU-X, or Toyota Commuter)
  4.    Destination (i.e., The S31 Sukhumvit Hotel)

             Their staff will confirm you back with a quoted price (from 1000-1400 baht) and the instruction to their counter when you arrive at the Suvannabhumi airport. Please note that there could be wait time from 15-30 minutes since they serve customers by arrival time. Payment can be made in cash or credit card at the counter.

By Taxi

 Service: 24 Hours

Proceed to the taxi counter on level-1 at the Arrivals Hall, gates 4 and 7, The journey will take around 30 to 50 minutes depending on the traffic. Fare charged according to the meter (distance travelled), expressway fees, and airport surcharge of THB 50.

 By Airport Rail Link

Service: 06.00 - 24.00  (for BTS, MRT, and Airport Rail Link)

Airport Rail Link is located at the basement level of Suvarnabhumi Airport. It is recommended to purchase a ticket to Makkasan station, change the line and take the subway from Phetchaburi MRT Station to Sukhumvit MRT Station. On arrival at Sukhumvit MRT Station take Exit No. 3, and change the line to BTS. Travel from Asok BTS Station to Phrom Phong BTS Station to reach the hotel.

 
  • City Line’s fare between Makkasan Station to the airport is THB 35 (stops 6 stations along the way, takes around 30 minutes).
  • BTS and MRT interchange stations are not directly connected. You may need to take a 5-minute walk to change from MRT to BTS station.
  • BTS, MRT, and Airport Rail Link have different payment systems. You will need to purchase a ticket for each line.
